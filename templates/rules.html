Game Rules

Objective of the Game

The goal is to accumulate more points than the dealer without exceeding 21 points. Each round presents new challenges, requiring strategic decision-making!

Game Setup

  • Each player receives two cards face up.
  • The dealer receives two cards: one face up, one face down.
  • The player with the highest total points begins the game.

Card Values

  • Number Cards (2-10): Worth their face value.
  • Face Cards (Jack, Queen, King): Worth 10 points.
  • Aces: Worth either 1 or 11 points, depending on the player's hand.

Gameplay Mechanics

During your turn:

  • Hit: Draw a card to increase your total points.
  • Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn.

If your total exceeds 21 points, you bust and lose the round.

Dealer's Turn

  • The dealer reveals their face-down card.
  • The dealer must hit until they have at least 17 points.
  • If the dealer busts, all remaining players win the round.

Winning the Round

  • If your points are higher than the dealer's without busting, you win.
  • If the dealer busts, all players still in the game win.
  • If both you and the dealer have the same total, it's a push, and your bet is returned.

Scoring

Points are awarded for each round won. Keep track of your total score to determine the overall winner at the end of the game.

Tips for Success

  • Consider the dealer's face-up card when deciding to hit or stand.
  • Know when to take risks and when to play it safe.
  • Practice to improve your skills and strategy.
